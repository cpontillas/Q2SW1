Cast of Frozen

Character Information Quote

Elsa		 Elsa, the Snow Queen, is one of the protagonists of the Frozen franchise. Born with the power of
ice and snow, Elsa is the firstborn daughter of King Agnarr and Queen Iduna, the older sister of
Queen Anna, and the former queen of Arendelle.		 " You can't marry a man you just met. "

Anna		 Queen Anna of Arendelle is one of the protagonists of the Frozen franchise. She is the
second-born daughter of King Agnarr and Queen Iduna, the younger sister of Elsa the
Snow Queen, and the contemporary ruler of Arendelle.		 " Do you want to build a snowman ? "

Kristoff		 Kristoff Bjorgman is the tritagonist of the Frozen franchise. He is a Sami outdoorsman
that worked as an ice harvester with his reindeer Sven. Having been an orphan who
was raised by a colony of trolls in the mountains, Kristoff was initially wary of humans,
believing them to be untrustworthy, cheating crooks.		 " Reindeers are better than people. "

Olaf		 Olaf is a major character in the Frozen franchise. He is a benevolent, enchanted
snowman created by Elsa. Along with the ability to reassemble his body, Olaf’s
most prominent quirk is his love for summer and all things hot.		 " Some people are worth melting for. "

Prince Hans		 Prince Hans of the Southern Isles is the main antagonist of Frozen. As the 13th-born
prince of the Southern Isles, Hans was burdened with the inability to ascend his family’s
throne. Desperate for power and recognition, he sought to marry Princess Anna of
Arendelle with the purpose of usurping the throne of Queen Elsa and taking over as king.		 " I've been searching my whole life to find
my own place. "
